About Me

My journey through poetry.

Having retired from the teaching profession after thirty years of instructing middle school students in French, Mathematics and English, I've now found extra time to devote to my life-long passion of poetry writing.

Since early childhood when I was introduced to A.A Milne’ poetry, I have always been drawn to rhyming and rhythmic verse – and I still gravitate towards this genre in my own writing. A prolific poet during my primary school years, I have continued to find poetry an excellent medium for recording the observations, particular moments and feelings experienced in the course of our everyday lives.

Although I have written poems on a diverse range of themes, the bulk of my creative energy finds an outlet in the hundreds of poems I have written through the eyes of a child. Given that childhood is such a short and precious phase in the human life cycle, I feel drawn to immortalising the ups and downs of being a child in a way that can be easily and fondly remembered.
