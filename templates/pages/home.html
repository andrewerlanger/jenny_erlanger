About Me

I've been writing poetry for as long as I can remember.

I still have an exercise book containing several of the poems I wrote as a child and over the years I've realised that the inner child is still there and itching to have its voice heard.

Although many things have changed since my own childhood, my passion for capturing life's experiences in rhyming verse has been a constant.

It's time to let that inner child out to play for a while.
